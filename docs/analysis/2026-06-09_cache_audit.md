# Cache Audit — what's on disk, what's the stack, what we may reuse (2026-06-09)

**Trigger:** before the full standard run, verify no stale/wrong-config detection cache gets silently
reused. **Verdict: rebuild everything fresh at the standard budget — reuse NOTHING by skip-if-exists.**

## The one reassurance that removes most of the worry
**The trust-classifier (robust8) and the verifiers (mlp_v5 / mlp_v5_ir_aligned) are NEVER baked into a
cache** — they are applied *live at replay* (`joblib.load(ROBUST8_JBL)` + MLP forward on cached feats).
So **no cache can carry a "wrong classifier"** — it's whatever the script loads at scoring time
(currently the shipped robust8, eval-only). The ONLY thing a cache can get wrong is **detector + imgsz +
frame budget** (those are baked at cache-build time).

## Why "don't reuse" matters operationally (the trap)
Both current harnesses **skip a surface whose `.pkl` already exists** (`pipeline_cache.py:143`,
`compare_routing_pipeline.py:296`). So if the standard run is launched **without `--overwrite`**, it will
**silently reuse the n=1000 mini caches** → thesis numbers at the wrong frame budget. **The standard run
MUST pass `--overwrite`** (both harnesses have the flag) or the current-dir caches must be deleted first.

## What was learned / corrected
- The big "full antiuav + svanstrom" cache (`_email_recompute`, 58 shards, ~470 MB) is **not** a stale
  wrong-model cache as first assumed — it is **ft4 + v3b at the correct standard imgsz (antiuav 640!,
  svan 1280)**, full set, conf-floor 0.10. It's just on a different (paired) harness.
- **Anti-UAV @640 already exists** in three places (email full, offline mini, routing mini) — the only
  thing still @1280 is the *thesis's old Stage-1 table*, regenerated by the standard run.
- The two `_overnight_ablation*` dirs are the only ones whose **detector cannot be verified from meta** —
  treat as superseded, do not reuse.

## Recommendation
Rebuild all 8 canonical surfaces **fresh** at the §0 standard, on the offline + routing harnesses, with
**`--overwrite`**. Detectors (ft4/v3b) and the live classifier/verifier are already correct, so a clean
run is correct by construction. Optionally delete/move the 4 stale dirs (`_overnight_ablation`,
`_overnight_ablation_full`, `_routing_pipeline_cmp_bak_20260605`, and the mini caches) to remove the
skip-reuse trap — **on your green light** (won't touch them otherwise).
